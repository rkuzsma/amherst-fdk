Our children need your support.

Amherst citizens, our children need your support to vote YES on Article 13 and 12.

Bring Full Day Kindergarten to Amherst elementary schools, and approve the school budget.

Where: Souhegan High School

When: Tuesday, March 10. Anytime between 6:00am and 8:00pm

What is proposed?

Article 13 will provide tuition-free, full day kindergarten for all Amherst kindergarteners.

Why Full Day Kindergarten?

Today's Kindergarteners are facing higher expectations than ever before. ... (get some writeup here). Teachers need time to teach and reinforce.

• Nationally, 82% of all districts offer full day kindergarten. National education standards assume most kindergarteners have already had a full day curriculum.

• 87+ NH districts already offer fully funded kindergarten. We want to keep Amherst a destination for families who want to raise kids in a great school district.

Don't reflect upon your own experience as a Kindergartener. It's not the same world; expectations today are far higher.
