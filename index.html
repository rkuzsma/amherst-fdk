The New First Grade

Kindergarteners today face:
o More focus on rigorous academic skills
o Less focus on music, art and creative activities
o Higher academic and behavioral expectations when they enter kindergarten

Kindergartners need a full day.

"Full Day K" Means:

o More time to practice and internalize social and academic skills
o More time for social interaction with peers
o Time for opportunities in the creative arts
o More time to engage in small group and individualized instruction
o Time to develop a stronger and interconnected learning community among students, teachers and parents
o Time to address the development of the whole child
