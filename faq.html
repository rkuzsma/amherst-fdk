Frequently Asked Questions

Where and when do I vote?

Vote at Souhegan High School on Tuesday, March 10, 2015.
Polls open at 6:00AM and close at 8:00PM.

If you're not a registered voter, you can register when you get there.

Your Vote Counts! Fewer than 34% of registered Amherst voters get out and vote. Some school budget articles in Amherst have previously passed by fewer than 50 votes. So please, come out and vote YES on Articles 13 and 12 to approve Full Day Kindergarten and the School Budget.

What and when is the Deliberative Session?

The Deliberative Session is an open forum for the community to learn about the articles that are up for a vote and optionally raise amendments to them.
The Deliberative session for these articles is on February 3 at Souhegan High School at 7:00pm.

On average, fewer than 2% of registered Amherst voters attend deliberative sessions. Please come, learn, and support our schools!
